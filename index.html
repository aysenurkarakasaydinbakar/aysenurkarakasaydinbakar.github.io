Researcher

Ayşenur
Karakaş Aydınbakar, PhD

Household Economics, Labor Economics, Gender

My research sits at the intersection of gender, household economics, and labor markets. I examine how families negotiate work and care, how divorce reshapes women's economic lives, and how employment patterns transmit across generations — with a focus on Türkiye and comparative evidence from Japan.
