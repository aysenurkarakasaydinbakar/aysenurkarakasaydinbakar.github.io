About

Ayşenur
Karakaş Aydınbakar

I completed my undergraduate degree in Economics in 2013 and went on to pursue a Master's at Istanbul Technical University. While there, I was awarded a doctoral scholarship by the Republic of Türkiye and moved to England in 2015. After a year and a half, I began my PhD at Tohoku University in September 2016, where I studied household economics and gender in the Japanese context.

I returned to Türkiye in 2019 after completing my doctorate and joined the Social Sciences University of Ankara, where I have been based ever since. Every two years, I return to Tohoku for month-long collaborative visits — a rhythm I've come to look forward to.

Outside of work, my days are shaped by reading, learning to code, doing research, and spending time with my family.
